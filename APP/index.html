Technologies

  • Distributed control rights management (DCRM)

    DCRM is the process that hands over the control of digital assets by that individuals or centralized organizations to decentralized blockchain’s management. The distributed generation and distributed storage of private key ensure that no single individual can access the complete private key, which means there is not a single node to obtain the control of digital assets in the state of distributed control rights management.

  • Multiple Triggering Mechanism (MTM)

    In addition to supporting the transaction triggering mechanism, timing and event triggering are also introduced in smart contract. The three triggering modes are formed by abstracting various financial application scenarios and can satisfy various types of requirements for smart contract trigger needs in complex cryptofinancial applications. This enables DSvr and DApp architecture.

  • Hierarchical Hybrid Consensus Mechanism (HHCM)

    HHCM realizes the balance between PoW and PoS, combines the advantages of both, avoids the centralized trend of computing power or stake and achieves a reasonable and stable energy efficiency ratio for FUSION system. HHCM realizes parallel processing of transactions, by built-in grouping parallel mechanism.

Community

The FUSION inclusive cryptofinance platform is a public blockchain. The FUSION Foundation, as a sponsor of the project, is working for the promising ecosystem of the blockchain rather than corporate profits, as traditional enterprise projects do. The FUSION platform, which benefits all token holders, does not belong to any single organization or individual and is a platform embracing the entire blockchain token community. It will use token mechanisms and the foundation to continually foster all kinds of communities such as technical communities, college communities, user communities, investment communities, node participant communities, central organizations, data source providers etc. The vison of FUSION community is to create an inclusive big family who loves FUSION.

Token Sale

  • 4)
    Selected participants Sale: 8,192,000 (10%) will be assigned to the token sale for ETH to selected participants to acquire other tokens to fund project development and team operations;
  • 5)
    Voluntary participants Sale: 20,480,000 (25%) will be allocated to the token sales for ETH to voluntary participants for the foundation to foster the ecological development of FUSION;
  • 6)

  • 7)

FAQ

  • A. What is Fusion?

    FUSION is a protocol level decentralized blockchain platform that allows digital asset (BTC, ETH etc.) holders to increase asset’s liquidity and leverage various financial services.

  • B. What is Fusion Foundation?

    The Foundation’s statutory purpose is nurturing and stewarding cutting-edge technologies and applications in the field of cryptographically-powered decentralized protocols. The Foundation’s principal focus is research, development, deployment and maintenance of cryptofinance technologies, comprising the "Fusion" cryptofinance protocol, the “Microservice” smart-contract platform and the hierarchy hybrid consensus mechanism and their various support technologies, in addition to advocacy and education, developer-adoption, support of middleware and base-layer/demonstration applications relating Fusion protocol set.
    The Foundation Council is the governing body responsible for the management of the Foundation.

  • C. What is Foundation’s relationship with FUSION?

    The Foundation intends to use funds raised through the sale of Fusion(FSN) token to finance the development of cryptofinance Technologies, including FUSION. To this end, the Foundation intends to work closely with third parties. The Foundation intends to partner with many financial service providers to abroad their services on FUSION.

  • D. What is FSN and it’s value?

    FSN is the token used within FUSION platform.
    I. The gas of smart contract.
    II. The stake used in the layer 1 Proof of Stake consensus mechanism.
    III. The reward to the miners of both Proof of Stake and Proof of Workload consensus mechanism.
    IV. The transaction fees within FUSION.

  • E. What Problem does Fusion solved?

    The endeavors today are mostly only focusing on enhancing current financial institutions’ internal systems with blockchain technology. FUSION create a new system: a value transfer infrastructure based on tokens. It can convert values across time and across space and can fulfill almost all the functions of traditional finance in a distributive way much more efficiently and much less costly. It can even achieve many unprecedented financial functions that cannot be imagined in scenarios of centralized organizations.

  • F. Can a participant’s FSNs be reallocated or deleted?

    Certain active participant behavior may result in a punitive reduction of that participant’s security bond (through deletion or reallocation) or a reduction in the amount of their FSN reward. The extent of the punishment will depend on the misbehavior in question and will be pre-determined by the protocol.

  • G. How will FSNs be allocated?

    Refer to FSN allocation plan.

  • H. When will I receive FSNs?

    After the ICO, participants will receive FSN ERC20 tokens. Upon a deployment of the Fusion genesis block, participants can lock in the Fusion ERC20 token on the FUSION platform, then the FSNs will be allocated to the FUSION account of which these ERC20 token were locked in.

  • I. Will FSNs be valuable?

    The value of FSNs is depends on the digital asset holder and financial services provides on the FUSION. Thinking about do you want exchange service without giving your asset to centralized company. Do you want make benefit from your digital asset without selling it?

  • J. Are FSNs going to be another crypto currency?

    It is not the intention of the Foundation for FSNs to be used as a crypto currency. The intention of the Foundation is to complete the current crypto currency ecosystem with crypto finance services. We are here to complete rather than compete.

  • K. What is the 5% reserve in the token distribution plan for?

    In order to make a better use of the FSN token, a 5% reserve can give the Foundation more discretion to foster the ecosystem of FUSION in the future. The Foundation may make a decision of the use of the reserve based on future scenarios such as:

    The Foundation may need more FSN token for token exchange with other blockchain tokens or values in the centralized organizations;

    The Foundation may need more ETH tokens for the development of FUSION;

    The bookkeeping nodes may need more incentives to do the work safely and efficiently.
