Technologies

  • Distributed control rights management (DCRM)

    DCRM is the process that hands over the control of digital assets by that individuals or centralized organizations to decentralized blockchain’s management. The distributed generation and distributed storage of private key ensure that no single individual can access the complete private key, which means there is not a single node to obtain the control of digital assets in the state of distributed control rights management.

  • Multiple Triggering Mechanism (MTM)

    In addition to supporting the transaction triggering mechanism, timing and event triggering are also introduced in smart contract. The three triggering modes are formed by abstracting various financial application scenarios and can satisfy various types of requirements for smart contract trigger needs in complex cryptofinancial applications. This enables DSvr and DApp architecture.

  • Hierarchical Hybrid Consensus Mechanism (HHCM)

    HHCM realizes the balance between PoW and PoS, combines the advantages of both, avoids the centralized trend of computing power or stake and achieves a reasonable and stable energy efficiency ratio for FUSION system. HHCM realizes parallel processing of transactions, by built-in grouping parallel mechanism.

  • D. What is FSN and it’s value?

    FSN is the token used within Fusion platform.
    I. The gas of smart contract.
    II. The stake used in the layer 1 Proof of Stake consensus mechanism.
    III. The reward to the miners of both Proof of Stake and Proof of Workload consensus mechanism.
    IV. The transaction fees for transactions within Gluon.

  • E. What Problem does Fusion solved?

    The endeavors today are mostly only focusing on enhancing current financial institutions’ internal systems with blockchain technology. FUSION create a new system: a value transfer infrastructure based on tokens. It can convert values across time and across space and can fulfill almost all the functions of traditional finance in a distributive way much more efficiently and much less costly. It can even achieve many unprecedented financial functions that cannot be imagined in scenarios of centralized organizations.

  • F. Can a participant’s FSNs be reallocated or deleted?

    Certain active participant behavior may result in a punitive reduction of that participant’s security bond (through deletion or reallocation) or a reduction in the amount of their FSN reward. The extent of the punishment will depend on the misbehavior in question and will be pre-determined by the protocol.

  • G. How will FSNs be allocated?

    Refer to FSN allocation plan.

  • H. When will I receive FSNs?

    After the ICO, participants will receive FSN ERC20 tokens. Upon a deployment of the Fusion genesis block, participants can lock in the Fusion ERC20 token on the FUSION platform, then the FSNs will be allocated to the FUSION account of which these ERC20 token were locked in.

  • I. Will FSNs be valuable?

    The value of FSNs is depends on the FUSION platform. The more digital asset holder used FUSION, the more financial services provides on the FUSION, the more value will FSN have.

  • J. Are FSNs going to be another crypto currency?

    It is not the intention of the Foundation for FSNs to be used as a crypto currency. The intention of the Foundation is to complete the current crypto currency ecosystem with crypto finance services. We are here to complete rather than compete.
